usage: main.py [-h] [-s ALARM_SOUND] [-a ALARM]
               [--escape-quits | --no-escape-quits]

Simple visual timer. Play a sound after timer(s) count down.

options:
  -h, --help            show this help message and exit
  -s, --alarm-sound ALARM_SOUND
                        Audio file to be played back, including this argument
                        multiple times will result in per-timer alarm sound
  -a, --alarm ALARM     Alarm duration. e.g. '4h 30m 20s'. Numbers without
                        units like '100' will be interpreted as 100 seconds
  --escape-quits, --no-escape-quits
                        Should the escape key quit this app
